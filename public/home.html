OTMANIA

Learn cybersecurity in operational technologies through interactive challenges, real-world scenarios, and strategic decision-making.

About the Game

OTMANIA is a strategy and simulation game where you manage cybersecurity in an industrial environment. Solve tasks, respond to threats, and keep operations running safely.

Gameplay Experience

In OTMANIA, you take control of a cybersecurity operator responsible for keeping an industrial plant safe. As time progresses, challenges increase and your ability to react quickly becomes critical.

Tasks

Perform daily cybersecurity operations such as configuring systems, analyzing logs, and managing industrial devices.

Alerts

React to real-time threats like ransomware, DDoS attacks, and system breaches before they escalate.

Decision Making

Every choice matters. Correct actions increase your score, while mistakes can compromise the system.

Progression

The game becomes more challenging over time, reducing response windows and increasing complexity.

Real Industry Inspiration

Step into a real industrial environment inspired by Rockwell Automation, where every decision can protect — or compromise — critical systems.
